About AlphaPulse

Live market data, built solo, for free.

AlphaPulse is a free, real-time stock research tool — live prices, market news, technical indicators, sector performance, and an investment simulator, all in one place. No login, no paywall, no app to download.

It's an independent side project built by one person who wanted a fast, clean way to check the market without ads cluttering every page or a dozen tabs open at once.

Where the data comes from

Live prices and news come from Finnhub. Historical charts and technical indicators come from Alpha Vantage. Both are independent, third-party financial data providers — AlphaPulse simply displays what they report.

What AlphaPulse is not

AlphaPulse is an informational tool, not a financial advisor, broker-dealer, or registered investment service. Nothing shown in the app — prices, technical indicators, the investment simulator, or anything else — should be treated as personalized financial advice or a recommendation to buy or sell anything.

How it stays free

AlphaPulse may include affiliate links to brokers and trading tools. If you sign up through one of those links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects what data is shown or how it's presented.
