Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

AlphaPulse doesn't have a backend server, doesn't require an account, and doesn't collect or store your personal data on any server we control. Almost everything happens directly in your browser.

What's stored, and where

If you connect a Finnhub or Alpha Vantage API key, add tickers to your watchlist, or set a price alert, that information is saved using your browser's local storage (localStorage) — on your device only. It is never transmitted to us, because we don't operate a server that receives it.

Third-party data providers

When you use AlphaPulse, your browser makes direct requests to:

  • Finnhub — for live prices, quotes, and news
  • Alpha Vantage — for historical price data

Those requests are made using the API key you provide, directly from your browser to their servers. Their own privacy policies govern how they handle that traffic — we'd encourage you to review them.

Cookies

AlphaPulse itself does not use tracking cookies. If we add analytics or advertising in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect that before any such feature goes live.

Affiliate links

Some pages include affiliate links to third-party brokers or trading tools. Clicking one may set a tracking cookie controlled by that third party, not by AlphaPulse, so that they can credit the referral if you sign up.

Children's privacy

AlphaPulse is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a backend, analytics, or login system is added later — this page will be updated and the "last updated" date above will change.

Contact

Questions about this policy? See our Contact page.
